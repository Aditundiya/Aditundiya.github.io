Comet Loop Privacy Policy

Effective date: 14 June 2026

Comet Loop ("the app", "we") is a casual puzzle game. This policy explains how the app handles data.

The short version
  • The game works fully offline. Your progress (levels, stars, settings, skins, streak) is stored only on your device and is never uploaded by us.
  • We do not ask for, collect, or store your name, email, contacts, location, or any account.
  • When your device is online, the app shows advertisements served by Google AdMob. When you are offline, no ads are shown and no ad data is processed.

Data stored on your device

Game progress, star balance, unlocked skins, daily streak, and settings are saved in the app's local storage on your device only. Uninstalling the app deletes this data. We have no copy of it and no way to access it.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

When the device has an internet connection, the app displays ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process data — such as the device advertising ID, approximate location derived from IP address, app activity, and ad-interaction events — to serve and measure ads. This collection is performed by Google, not by us.

  • Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • How Google uses data from partner apps: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites
  • You can reset or delete your advertising ID and opt out of ad personalisation in Android Settings → Privacy → Ads.

In the EEA/UK, the app shows a Google-certified consent (UMP) message before serving personalised ads. When the device is offline, no ad requests are made.

Children

Comet Loop is content-appropriate for all ages and rated Everyone, with a target audience of 13 and older. The app does not request any personal information from any user. Ad content is limited to a General audience rating.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new effective date.
